RULES AND REGULATIONS

This quiz is designed in various levels. The main goal is to reach the next level by solving the riddle.
Each level has one problem to be solved. Solving it takes you to new level
If your answer is wrong, you remain on the same level. And that simply means, TRY HARDER to get to the answer!!!
People skipping levels will be disqualified (Yes, we do know!!).

Caps and spaces are not allowed.

Eg: if your answer is I love Kryptex, you will have to enter ilovekryptex to proceed to the next level.
Beware of the spelling you enter, Kryptex can't auto correct your spellings.s

Only entries in ascending order for all levels will be accepted.
